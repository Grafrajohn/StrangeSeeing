Kelly Hopkinsville Incident

Graham

In the quiet hamlet of Kelly, south western Kentucky on the evening of August 21st 1955, the Sutton family found themselves to be hosts to some uninvited visitors to their farm.

The Suttons were not the kind of people to engage in fanciful story telling; they were simple folk who relied on hard work, and when needed, their guns.

At about 7pm a friend of the family saw a silvery flying object, which left a multi-coloured exhaust trail, pass over the farm house. It then landed nearby. He reported his sighting to the Suttons who dismissed and indeed ridiculed it.

A while later the Sutton family became aware of a glow on the ground near their farmhouse and the presence in the midst of it of small humanoid creatures as illustrated above. Frightened, a couple of the men grabbed their guns and opened fire on the creature. Apparently it fell over and fled but seemed to be unharmed.

As the evening progressed the several of the strange creatures came closer to the house and began to peer through the windows. Once gain the Suttons opened fire on the creatures, which were once again apparently unharmed.

When the incidents occurred none of the Sutton family had been drinking and indeed alcohol was not allowed in the family farmhouse.
